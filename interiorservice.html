INTERIOR DESIGN CONSULTANCY SERVICES

Interior Design Consultancy Services & Scope

  • We are pleased to be the Interior Designers for your Residence
  • We will be offering you the following services:
  • ● Formulation of Requirements.
  • ● Preparation of Schematic interior Overall Layouts based on requirements.
  • ● Feedback from the client and incorporating them in the overall Scheme drawings.
  • ● Preparation of individual layout in a scheme manner.
  • ● Client feedback and incorporating the feedback and finalization of the Scheme
  • ● Issue of Scheme drawings for living, dining, bedrooms, toilets, etc.
  • ● Issue of “Good for Construction” drawings for all the interior areas.
  • We will also assist to identify various materials like Veneer, Laminates, glass, handles, Accessories etc. Our services also include periodical supervision and visiting various shops for the identification of appropriate elements.
  • Interiors, generally consume lot of our professional time to be spent in a very short duration. We have to co-ordinate with multiple agencies and make them deliver very high quality finish.

Interior Design Consultancy Services & Scope

  • Interior Layouts of the Floor

  • Furniture Details
  • Electrical Layouts
  • False Ceiling Layout
